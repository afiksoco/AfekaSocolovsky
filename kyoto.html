Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto served as Japan's capital for over 1,000 years and is home to countless temples, shrines, and traditional wooden houses. The city is renowned for preserving Japanese culture and traditions, making it a window into Japan's rich historical heritage.

Visitors can explore over 2,000 temples and shrines, walk through the iconic Fushimi Inari's thousand red torii gates, and experience the tranquility of the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove. Kyoto is especially beautiful during cherry blossom season in spring and the vibrant fall foliage in autumn.
